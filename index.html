HOW DOES IT WORK?

The CòóP-Lens™ is a virtual reality (VR) lens that connects to the CòóP-Adventure™ app  and controllers (arm and leg strap) through bluetooth. The CòóP-Lens™ is intended to be visual environment like a big 360 degree screen in different mode of  games, with the controller sensing the motion and pressure on the straps to grade your exercises. Choose the favourite games from CòóP-Adventure™ app ans start playing the games by moving the body following the games instruction.

CòóP-Adventure™ has a virtual animation effect, it brings an individual to the world of animation. You can “meet up” with the characters in the animation games and play with them.

 CòóP-Adventure™ FAQ

Q: Can I enjoy CòóP-Adventure™ even if I don’t have much exercise experience?

Yes! Even if you’re not a workout junkie, it can be fun to fight enemies and progress through the game. You can manipulate the game’s intensity using the in-game settings to make compatible with your strength and ability.

Q: How did you come up with the movements used to control the game?

The exercises were developed with the help of certified fitness coach. The CòóP-Adventure™ includes over 50 types of real-life exercises. The in-game guide is always pop out at the screen as reminder to help you find proper posture for each exercise!

Q: What is do and don't for this game?

It is recommended to use no more than 1 hour of use as it uses VR technology. This is because you may experience headache and eye pain if used for a long time. In addition, the objects you see in the virtual environment do not occur in the real environment, so follow the guidelines and be careful about the surrounding of you.

Q: When sweating, does it effect the function of glasses?

Absolutely not because the frame is designed specifically for water resistance. Furthermore, we will give you 1 year warranty on the product based on applicable terms and conditions.
